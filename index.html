Pensive

September 25, 2025 by Jeremy

Patrick Star once said "The inner machinations of my mind are an enigma". I believe that this very profound quote sums up the bizarre nature of how I think as person. It's as if thousands of thoughts continuously race to form a sentence but they either all reach at the same time which leads to a jumbled mess or they never get there. Even while writing this first paragraph it took me 30 minutes to fully realize what I wanted to say and how I should put it.

Maybe I unintentionally filter myself due to me thinking that my writing has to be on par with some convoluted academic standard so that I can receive a good grade. But, upon further analysis, the instructions didn't tell me what I can and can't write so here I am; voicing what I currently think. I don't even know if I used that semi-colon correctly but it's there. I did go on a bit of a tangent but the point is... I am not sure if there really was one but it did feel good to write without constraints.

Filler Arc

September 25, 2025 by Jeremy

A filler, specifically in media, refers to TV show episodes that do not advance the main plot or develop the characters and exist primarily to "fill" the required episode count or give the original source material more time to generate more content.

This is a quite common anime trope in shows such as Bleach, One Piece and Naruto. However, in regard to this assignment, this entire paragraph is used to fill up the required space to show wrapping of the used photo. Frankly, this is my version of the "Lorem ipsum dolor..." placeholder text. Thank you for your time.
